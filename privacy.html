Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

Tarot With AI ("the App") is operated by the developer ("we", "us"). This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Account information: When you sign up, we collect your email address and display name. If you use Google Sign-In, we receive your Google account email and unique identifier.

Usage data: We collect information about your tarot readings (spread type, cards drawn, AI interpretations) to provide the service.

Purchase data: In-app purchase transactions are processed by Google Play and RevenueCat. We store transaction IDs and product identifiers to credit your account.

Device information: We collect your device locale and timezone to provide localized content.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve the tarot reading service
  • Generate personalized AI interpretations
  • Process purchases and manage your in-app currency balance
  • Send daily reward notifications (if enabled)
  • Maintain login streaks and account security

3. AI Processing

Your reading questions and card selections are sent to third-party AI services (Google Gemini, OpenAI) to generate interpretations. These services process your data according to their own privacy policies. We do not send your personal identity information (name, email) to AI providers.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • AI providers (Google, OpenAI) — reading context only, no personal identifiers
  • RevenueCat — purchase verification
  • Cloud infrastructure (AWS) — hosting and data storage

5. Data Retention

Your account data and reading history are retained as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time from the Settings screen, which permanently removes all your data.

6. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS), secure token storage, and hashed passwords to protect your data.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 18. We require age verification during signup and do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Your Rights

You may:

  • Access and export your reading history within the App
  • Edit your profile information
  • Delete your account and all associated data

9. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.
